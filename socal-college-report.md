# California Residency Considerations

If you intend to become a California resident after your first year:

* The tuition savings at UCSD and SDSU can be substantial.
* Simply living in California while attending college generally **does not** qualify you for in-state tuition.
* California residency for tuition purposes typically requires evidence that California has become your permanent home for reasons other than education, along with meeting residency and financial independence requirements.

Plan carefully if your financial strategy depends on qualifying for resident tuition.

# Final Thoughts

This list provides an excellent balance of public and private institutions while offering strong programs across engineering, business, sciences, healthcare, and liberal arts.

The most important factors that will determine admission success are:

1. GPA and course rigor
2. Quality of essays (where applicable)
3. Extracurricular depth and leadership
4. Intended major (especially for impacted programs)
5. Standardized test scores (only for schools where they remain optional)
